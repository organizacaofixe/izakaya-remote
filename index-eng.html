Izakaya Dining Room

The space transports the customer to a dining room with 12 seats, assuming the reservation for dinner time and without rotating tables in shifts. Those who visit us enjoy an innovative and more inclusive experience in the city through Japanese culture, Edomae style. The form, materiality and architectural concept coexist in unison with the execution of our dishes, allowing our customers to be involved in the experience we create for them.

Equipa Izakaya

​Friends since the age of 5, with a lifetime of travels linked to study and gastronomic experiences, which were essential in the creation of our first project. The passion for receiving, combined with the knowledge and emotion for Japanese culture, led us to open Izakaya precisely on the street where we played since elementary school days.

Menu

The product is purchased daily by the number of reservations we have, as we do not have a freezer in our space, which allows us to create changeable menus.

We favor all locally sensitive products, we use fish from our coast and we directly import products from Japan that cannot be purchased in the western world. We use the dry-aging technique to recreate the ancestry of Japanese cuisine and improve the product, especially the fish.

Our entire menu can be adapted to vegetarians, and to different types of intolerances, upon notice at the time of booking.
